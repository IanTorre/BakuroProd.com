Introduction

Bakuro Production Team was once "A dream."" A lot of things happened and is still happening. They say a lot of people come and go. But then.. They leave nothing but memories. Memories that once became
True to life, Unforgotten and Supreme.
At some point we may had thought about it. About what if, what if dreams and memories became a dragonfly,.... a dragonfly that is happily flying by, what if it became a bird that is happily singing by, what if it became a firefly that glows bright at night. Just what if, what if we could just capture it and live with it. Dreams that we gave up on, Memories that we cannot live on, Honor that had been flushed into nothingness. Just what if.
